Happy Place

The Happy Place is one of Noraml's local house DIY venues where our main goal is to bring artists and bands to the ISU campus at an affordable cost. We have been lucky enough to host a ton of local bands, as well as hearing acts from Iowa, Wisconsin, and Chicago. We strive to provide a fun accepting environment for students who enjoy live music.
