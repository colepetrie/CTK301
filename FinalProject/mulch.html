Mulch

     Mulch is a local ISU band who recently got their start earlier this semester. Beginning as an acoustic duo, the band has since progressed into a full rock band, drawing inspiriations from a variety of alternative rock music like TOOL, Led Zepplin, Seether, Blind Melon, and many more. Mulch has played all over Illinois State University including a variety of house venues and the Coffeehouse. We will soon be hitting the road to other cities in the near future.
